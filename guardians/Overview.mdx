## Getting Started

As a guardian, you will receive a welcome SMS or email once the school enrolls your child and links you to their profile.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive Your Credentials">
    The school will send you a temporary password and a link to the login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Enter your registered phone number or email and the temporary password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch Between Siblings">
    If you have multiple children in the school, you can easily switch between their profiles using the child selector at the top of your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fees and Payments

Stay on top of your school fee obligations with easy viewing and instant payments.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Viewing Invoices">
    1. Navigate to the **Fees** section.
    2. View current and past invoices for each child.
    3. Download PDF copies for your records.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Making Payments">
    You can pay fees instantly using **M-Pesa STK Push**:
    1. Click **Pay Now** on an outstanding invoice.
    2. Enter your phone number and confirm the amount.
    3. Enter your M-Pesa PIN on your phone to complete the transaction.
    4. Your invoice balance will update immediately.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Academic Progress

Monitor your child's journey through the term.

- **Real-Time Attendance**: Get notified immediately if your child is marked absent from class.
- **Assessment Results**: View scores for CATs and exams as soon as they are published by the teacher.
- **Digital Report Cards**: Download and print official term-end report cards directly from the portal.

## Communication

- **School Notices**: Receive urgent updates about school closures, events, and parents' days.
- **Newsletter Downloads**: Access school newsletters and termly circulars.
- **Update Your Profile**: Ensure your contact information is always up to date so you never miss an important message.

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password. How do I reset it?">
    On the login page, click **Forgot Password**. Enter your registered phone number or email, and we will send you a reset link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see one of my children?">
    If you have a child in the school who doesn't appear in your portal, please contact the school administration to ensure your guardian profile is correctly linked to that student's admission number.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my payment secure?">
    Yes. All M-Pesa transactions are processed securely through Safaricom. Elimu Bora does not store your M-Pesa PIN.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
