## Academic Reports

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Report Category">
    Go to **Reports → Academic**. Choose from **Merit Lists**, **Grade Summaries**, or **Subject Performance**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Filters">
    Filter by Academic Year, Term, Grade Level, and Stream.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export and Print">
    Generate the report as a PDF for school board meetings or CSV for custom analysis in Excel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Financial Reports

Stay on top of your school's cash flow.

- **Collections Report**: See exactly how much has been paid today, this week, or this term.
- **Balances Report**: Identify which students have outstanding fees and by how much.
- **Aging Report**: See how long fee balances have been outstanding (e.g., 30, 60, or 90\+ days).

## Attendance Insights

Identify patterns in student and staff attendance.

- **Heatmaps**: A visual representation of when students are most likely to be late or absent.
- **Comparison Reports**: Compare attendance rates between different streams or grade levels.

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I customize the reports?">
    While the standard reports cover 95% of school needs, you can export any report to **CSV/Excel** to perform your own custom filtering and calculations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why does a student appear in the report but they have already left?">
    Check if the student's status is set to **Active**. Archived or Graduated students are excluded from active term reports by default.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
