## How to set up your school

1. Receive your account. The Elimu Bora team emails your subdomain and Super Admin login.
2. Log in at `https://yourschool.elimuboraerp.com` and change your password.
3. Enable MFA on the Super Admin account from your profile.
4. Configure school details in Settings → School Details (name, motto, contact, logo, primary colour). See [School details](/settings/school-details).
5. Set up curriculum — pick CBC or 8-4-4, add grade levels, streams, subjects. See [Curriculum overview](/modules/curriculum/overview).
6. Create the academic year and terms. See [Academic years](/modules/academic-years/overview).
7. Add staff in Users. Assign roles (Teacher, Bursar, Class Teacher, etc.). See [Add a user](/modules/users/add-user).
8. Enrol students and link guardians. Use bulk import for large lists. See [Bulk import](/modules/users/bulk-import).
9. Activate modules based on your subscription plan (Attendance, Finance, Inventory, etc.).

## Notes

- The Super Admin role is unrestricted — assign carefully.
- Logos work best as transparent PNGs.
- Module availability depends on your plan. Contact the Elimu Bora team to upgrade.

## Troubleshooting

- Welcome email missing? Check spam, then contact support@elimuboraerp.com.
- Subdomain not loading? Confirm the URL has no typos and uses `https://`.
- Can't change password? Use the same browser tab and ensure password meets the strength rule (8+ chars, mixed case, number).
