Setting up Elimu Bora is a straightforward process. This guide will take you from your initial welcome email to running your first daily register.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initialize Your Subdomain">
    After signing up, you will receive a welcome email with your unique school URL (e.g., `https://yourschool.elimuboraerp.com`) and your **Super Admin** login credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure School Identity">
    Log in and navigate to **Settings**. Upload your school logo, set your official contact details, and choose your primary school color. These will appear on all system-generated documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Your Academic Structure">
    1. Define your **Grade Levels** and **Streams** in the Curriculum module.
    2. Add your **Academic Year** and define the specific **Terms**.
    3. Assign **Subjects** to each grade level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Onboard Users">
    Add your staff members and assign their roles. For students and guardians, we recommend using the **Bulk Import** tool to upload your existing records from a CSV file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Daily Operations">
    Assign teachers to their classes and subjects. Your school is now ready to begin marking attendance and recording fees!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Configuration Checkpoints

- **Logos**: Use high-quality transparent PNGs for the best appearance on report cards.
- **M-Pesa**: If you use Paybill or STK Push, ensure your credentials are correctly configured in **Settings → Modules**.
- **Security**: We strongly recommend that all Administrative users enable **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** on their profiles.

<Tip>
  Need help with your initial data migration? Our support team can assist you in formatting your CSV files for a smooth bulk import.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

- **Login Issues**: If you haven't received your welcome email, check your spam folder or contact `support@elimuboraerp.com`.
- **Module Access**: If a module (like Finance or Inventory) is missing from your sidebar, ensure it is enabled in **Settings → Modules** and included in your subscription plan.
