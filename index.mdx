Elimu Bora (Swahili for "quality education") is a school management platform purpose-built for Kenyan schools. It replaces paper registers, manual cashbooks, and disconnected spreadsheets with a single, role-aware system that gives every stakeholder — principals, teachers, parents, and support staff — the right information at the right time.

## How Elimu Bora works

Elimu Bora is a multi-tenant platform — each school runs on its own isolated subdomain (e.g., `yourschool.elimuboraerp.com`) with its own data and users. Your school's subscription plan determines which modules are available.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Your school is provisioned">
    The Elimu Bora team creates your school account and sends you a Super Admin login for your subdomain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your school">
    Set your school name, logo, primary colour, and academic structure (curriculum, grade levels, and streams).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your team and students">
    Create user accounts for teachers, staff, and guardians, then enrol students and link them to their guardians.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using modules">
    Begin taking attendance, running assessments, sending fee invoices, and managing your school — all in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available modules

Your plan determines which modules are active. Core modules are always available; optional modules are enabled based on your subscription.

| Module | Included in |
|---|---|
| Academics (assessments, report cards) | Starter, Base, Full |
| Attendance | Starter, Base, Full |
| Finance (invoices, M-Pesa, wallets) | Base, Full |
| Events | Base, Full |
| Inventory & Procurement | Full |
| Timetables | Full |
| Sports & Clubs | Full |

<Note>
  Contact the Elimu Bora team to upgrade your plan or enable additional modules for your school.
