Elimu Bora is a unified ERP platform designed specifically for Kenyan educational institutions running the **CBC** or **8-4-4** curricula. By digitizing daily operations—from attendance and academic grading to M-Pesa fee collection—we help schools eliminate paperwork and focus on student success.

## Who uses Elimu Bora

Elimu Bora is a school management platform built for Kenyan schools running CBC or 8-4-4. It unifies academics, attendance, finance, inventory, communication, and guardian access in one system.

Each school runs on an isolated subdomain (e.g. `yourschool.elimuboraerp.com`) and uses role-based access — what each user sees depends on the role assigned to them.

\[Insert screenshot: Elimu Bora dashboard home page after login, showing top navigation and module cards\]

## Who uses Elimu Bora

Our platform is designed for the entire school community:

- **School Leadership**: Principals and Directors use the dashboard for real-time oversight of finances and academic progress.
- **Teachers**: Mark registers, enter assessment scores, and manage their class timetables.
- **Administrators**: Bursars, storekeepers, and HR managers handle invoicing, procurement, and staff records.
- **Guardians**: Track their children's progress, receive alerts, and pay fees via M-Pesa.
- **Students**: Access their own results, timetables, and school notices.
