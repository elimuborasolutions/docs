Elimu Bora is a school management platform built for Kenyan schools running CBC or 8-4-4. It unifies academics, attendance, finance, inventory, communication, and guardian access in one system.

Each school runs on an isolated subdomain (e.g. `yourschool.elimuboraerp.com`) and uses role-based access — what each user sees depends on the role assigned to them.

[Insert screenshot: Elimu Bora dashboard home page after login, showing top navigation and module cards]

## Who uses Elimu Bora

- School leadership — principals, deputies, directors
- Teachers — class teachers, subject teachers, HODs
- Administrative staff — bursars, receptionists, librarians, HR
- Parents and guardians — view child records, fees, reports
- Students — access timetables, results, and communication

## Core modules

- Users & Identity
- Curriculum & Structure
- Academic Years & Terms
- Attendance
- Assessments & Report Cards
- Timetable & Lessons
- Events
- Finance
- Inventory & Procurement
- Sports & Games
- Clubs & Societies
- Activity Log
