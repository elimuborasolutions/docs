---
title: "Downloading Academic, Finance, and Inventory Reports"
description: "Generate and download reports for academics, finance, attendance, and inventory directly from Elimu Bora — no spreadsheets or manual counting required."
---

Elimu Bora generates reports across all the major areas of school management so you can review performance, track finances, and monitor stock without building spreadsheets manually. Reports are available in PDF or Excel format and are accessible from the Reports menu within each section of the system.

<Note>
  Large reports — such as inventory movements or full payment histories — are generated in the background. When the report is ready, you'll receive an in-app notification with a download link. For smaller reports, the download starts immediately.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Academics">
    Academic reports give you a clear view of student performance and assessment outcomes.

    **Report Cards**

    Each published report card is available as a PDF for individual students. The report card includes:
    - Grades for every subject in the assessment
    - Attendance figures for the assessment period
    - The student's class rank and position out of total students in the stream
    - Teacher and principal remarks

    To download a report card, go to **Academics > Report Cards**, find the student, and click **Download**.

    **Assessment Performance Overview**

    This report shows grade distribution at the class level, broken down by subject. Use it to identify subjects where many students are struggling or to compare performance across streams.

    To access it, go to **Academics > Reports > Assessment Performance Overview**.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Finance">
    Finance reports help you track fee collection, outstanding balances, and payment activity.

    **Invoice Summary**

    Shows a breakdown of all invoices for a selected term — how much has been paid versus how much is still outstanding. Use this as your end-of-term fee collection summary.

    **Payment History**

    A detailed record of every payment received, filterable by student or by fee type (billable). Use this to confirm specific payments or reconcile your fee records.

    To access finance reports, go to **Finance > Reports** and select the report you need.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Inventory">
    Inventory reports give you full visibility into your stock, purchasing, and supply chain activity.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Inventory Movements" icon="arrows-right-left">
        A full audit trail of every stock change — purchases, issues, returns, adjustments, and wastage — with before and after quantities recorded for each entry.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Purchase History" icon="shopping-cart">
        A chronological record of all purchases from suppliers, including quantities and order status.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Current Stock Levels" icon="cubes">
        A snapshot of how much stock you currently have for every product in your catalogue.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Stock Requisitions History" icon="clipboard-list">
        All internal stock requests raised by staff, including their approval status and outcome.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Low Stock Report" icon="triangle-exclamation">
        A list of all products currently below their configured reorder level, so you can act before stock runs out.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    To access inventory reports, go to **Inventory > Reports** and select the report you need.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Attendance">
    Attendance reports help you identify patterns and respond to absenteeism.

    **Register Summary**

    Shows present, absent, and late counts per class per day. Use this to review attendance trends across streams or to check a specific day's records.

    **Chronic Absenteeism**

    Lists students with repeated absences over a selected period. Use this report to identify students who need follow-up from the pastoral or welfare team.

    To access attendance reports, go to **Attendance > Reports** and select the report you need.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
