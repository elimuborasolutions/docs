title: "How Automatic Notifications and Alerts Work in Elimu Bora"
description: "Elimu Bora sends automatic in-app and email notifications for key school activities. No setup required — here's what to expect and how to view them."
---

Elimu Bora keeps every stakeholder informed without any manual work. When something important happens — a student is absent, an invoice is overdue, or an event is coming up — the system automatically sends a notification to the right person through two channels: in-app and email. You don't need to configure anything for notifications to work.

## Notification channels

**In-app notifications** appear inside Elimu Bora. Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation bar to open your notification panel. Unread notifications appear highlighted so you can spot them quickly.

**Email notifications** are delivered to the email address associated with your account. All emails come from `noreply@elimuboraerp.com`.

<Note>
  Add `noreply@elimuboraerp.com` to your contacts or email allowlist to prevent Elimu Bora notifications from being filtered into your spam or junk folder.
</Note>

## Viewing in-app notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation bar to open your notification list. Notifications are displayed in order of newest first. Unread notifications are highlighted. Click a notification to view the details or navigate to the related record.

## Email notifications

Email notifications arrive at the address registered on your account. Each email includes a summary of what happened and, where relevant, a link to view more details in Elimu Bora. Payment receipts include a downloadable PDF of the receipt directly in the email.
