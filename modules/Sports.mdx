## Sports Registry

Start by defining the sports and games your school participates in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a Sport">
    Go to **Sports** and click **New Sport**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Categorize">
    Specify whether it is an individual or team sport. You can also assign a "Head of Sport" staff member.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Team Management

Organize your students into competitive teams.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Team">
    Select a sport and click **New Team** (e.g., "Under 15 Football Team").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enroll Members">
    Search and add students to the team. You can assign roles like "Captain" or "Vice-Captain."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Coaches">
    Link staff members as coaches or managers for each team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fixtures and Results

Schedule fixtures for friendly matches or inter-school competitions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Schedule a Fixture">
    Go to **Fixtures** and select **New Fixture**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Details">
    Enter the date, time, location (Home/Away), and the opponent school.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record Results">
    After the match, update the fixture with the final score, match highlights, and any notable student performances.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can a student belong to multiple teams?">
    Yes, students can be members of multiple teams across different sports.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I track sports equipment?">
    Equipment tracking is handled via the **Inventory** module. Link your sports assets to the "Lab/Sports" category there.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
