## How to create an academic year

1. Go to **Settings → Academic Years**.
2. Click **New year**.
3. Enter the year name (e.g. `2026`) and start/end dates.
4. Click **Save**.
5. Click **Set active** to make it current.

[Insert screenshot: New academic year modal with name and date range fields]

## Notes

- Activating a new year does not delete the previous one.
- Add at least one term immediately so registers and assessments can run.

## Troubleshooting

- **Active year stuck?** Set another year active first, then archive the old one.
