Every record in Elimu Bora belongs to an academic year and term. You create the year first, then add its terms.

## Common tasks

- [Create an academic year](/modules/academic-years/create-year)
- [Manage terms](/modules/academic-years/terms)
- [Year-end rollover](/modules/academic-years/rollover)

## Notes

- Only one academic year is active at a time.
- Closing a year archives its data — reports remain accessible.

## Troubleshooting

- **Can't switch years?** Confirm the new year has at least one term defined.
