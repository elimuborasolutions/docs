Terms divide the academic year. Most Kenyan schools use three terms.

## How to add a term

1. Open the academic year.
2. Click the **Terms** tab.
3. Click **New term**.
4. Enter name (e.g. Term 1) and start/end dates.
5. Click **Save**.

## Notes

- Terms cannot overlap within the same year.
- Closing a term locks attendance and assessment edits for that term.

## Troubleshooting
- **Term won't close?** Confirm all assessments in that term are published or archived.
