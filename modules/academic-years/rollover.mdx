---
title: "Year-End Rollover"
description: "Promote students at year end and start a new year."
---

Rollover moves students up to the next grade and brings forward unpaid balances.

## How to roll over the year

1. Confirm all assessments and reports for the current year are published.
2. Go to **Settings → Academic Years → Rollover**.
3. Pick the new academic year.
4. Review the **Promotions** preview (each grade → next).
5. Adjust any individual student exceptions (repeats, transfers).
6. Click **Run rollover**.

## Notes

- Rollover is final — back up before running.
- Graduating students are archived automatically.
- Outstanding fees are carried over by default.

## Troubleshooting

- **Wrong grade after rollover?** Edit the student and use **Transfer**.
