## Marking Registers

Daily registers are automatically created for every class. Teachers can mark them directly from the dashboard or the Registers module.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Register">
    Navigate to **Registers** in the sidebar. Select the register for your class and the correct session (Morning or Afternoon).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark Student Status">
    For each student, select their status:
    - **Present**: The student is in class.
    - **Absent**: The student is not in class (automatically triggers a follow-up).
    - **Late**: The student arrived after the grace period.
    - **Early Leave**: The student left before the end of the session.
    - **Activity Leave**: The student is away for an approved school activity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the Register">
    Once all students are marked, click **Confirm Register** at the top of the page. This finalizes the records and sends automatic notifications to guardians of absent students.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You can use the **Mark All Present** button to quickly mark all students as present and then manually adjust any absentees.
</Info>

## Absence Follow-Ups

When a student is marked as **Absent** or **Late**, the system automatically creates a pending follow-up record to ensure the reason is documented.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Follow-Ups">
    Go to **Registers** and click on the **Follow-Ups** tab or navigate directly to the Follow-Ups section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document the Reason">
    Open the pending follow-up for a student. Select the reason provided by the guardian (e.g., Sick, Family Emergency, Transport Issue).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Notes & Evidence">
    Add any relevant notes and upload evidence if required (e.g., a medical note or a letter from the guardian).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close the Follow-Up">
    Click **Close Follow-Up** to mark the record as resolved. Resolved follow-ups are archived for auditing and reporting purposes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Staff Attendance

Staff members can sign in and out daily to track their attendance and punctuality.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Staff Sign-In">
    Staff members can sign in using their own devices or a designated school kiosk:
    1. Navigate to **Attendance → Staff**.
    2. Click **Sign In** when you arrive at school.
    3. Click **Sign Out** when leaving at the end of the day.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Management Review">
    Administrators can review and correct staff attendance records:
    1. Go to **Registers** and filter by staff.
    2. Use the **Edit** action to correct any missed sign-ins or sign-outs.
    3. View the **Attendance Heatmap** for a visual summary of staff punctuality.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Reports and Exports

Generate comprehensive reports to analyze attendance patterns across the school.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Report Type">
    Go to **Attendance → Reports** (or the Reports section in Registers). Choose from:
    - **Daily Snapshot**: A summary of today's attendance.
    - **Student Summary**: Total attendance percentage for individual students.
    - **Class/Stream Report**: Performance of a specific class over a period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter & Generate">
    Set the date range and filters (e.g., specific grade levels or streams) and click **Generate**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    Download the report as a **PDF** for printing or **CSV** for further analysis in Excel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Regularly export attendance summaries for Parent-Teacher meetings to discuss student consistency.
</Tip>

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I forget to mark a register?">
    The register will remain in a "Pending" state. Administrators will see a warning badge on their dashboard, and you will receive a notification to complete it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change a register after it's confirmed?">
    Once confirmed, a register is locked. To make changes, an administrator must **Reopen** the register first.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is a student missing from the register?">
    Check if the student is correctly assigned to the grade and stream. If they were recently admitted or moved, the register might need to be refreshed or their profile updated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are notifications sent for Late arrivals?">
    Yes, depending on your school settings, guardians can be notified for both Absences and Late arrivals.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
