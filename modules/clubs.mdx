## Club Registry

Set up the various clubs and societies offered by your school.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Club">
    Go to **Clubs** and click **New Club**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Leadership">
    Assign a **Patron** (staff member) and optionally record student leaders like the Club Chairperson or Secretary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Membership Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Members">
    Open a club's profile and navigate to the **Members** tab. You can add students individually or in bulk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Roles">
    Assign roles within the club to highlight student leadership responsibilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Meetings and Attendance

Track consistency and participation within each club.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Schedule a Meeting">
    Create a recurring or one-off meeting for the club.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark Attendance">
    During or after the meeting, the Patron can mark which members were present. This contributes to the student's holistic extra-curricular profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I charge club membership fees?">
    Club fees should be created as an **Optional Billable** in the **Finance** module. Once a student joins the club, assign that billable to them to include it in their termly invoice.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can students join clubs themselves?">
    Depending on your school settings, students may be able to request to join a club via their own portal, subject to approval by the Club Patron.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
