The Timetable module schedules lessons per stream across a typical school week. It supports recurring lessons and term-by-term renewal.

## Common tasks

- [Build a timetable](/modules/timetable/build-timetable)
- [Lessons](/modules/timetable/lessons)
- [Renew at term start](/modules/timetable/renew)

## Notes

- Each stream has its own timetable.
- Lessons can repeat weekly until the timetable expires.

## Troubleshooting

- **Empty timetable?** Build one for the active term first.
