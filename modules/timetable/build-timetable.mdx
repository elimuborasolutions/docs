## How to build a timetable

1. Go to **Timetable**.
2. Click **New timetable**.
3. Pick the term, grade, and stream.
4. Define periods (start/end times).
5. Assign each cell a subject and teacher.
6. Click **Save**.

## Notes

- Conflicts (same teacher, same time) are flagged before saving.
- Copy a timetable from a previous term to save time.

## Troubleshooting

- **Teacher conflict?** Reassign the lesson or move the period.
