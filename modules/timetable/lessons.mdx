A lesson is one cell in a timetable — a subject, teacher, room, and time slot.

## How to edit a lesson

1. Open the timetable.
2. Click the lesson cell.
3. Update teacher, room, or notes.
4. Click **Save**.

## Notes

- Changes affect future occurrences only by default.
- Use **Apply to this week only** to override one instance.
