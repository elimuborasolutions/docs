## How a staff member signs in

1. Open **Attendance → Staff** on a school device or your phone.
2. Tap **Sign in** for the day.
3. Tap **Sign out** when leaving.

[Insert screenshot: Staff sign-in screen with large Sign in button and today's status]

## How to review staff attendance

1. Go to **Attendance → Staff**.
2. Filter by date range, department, or staff member.
3. Click **Export** to download as CSV.

## Notes

- The HR/Secretary or Principal can correct records on behalf of staff.

## Troubleshooting

- **Can't sign out?** Refresh the page; your sign-in is preserved.
