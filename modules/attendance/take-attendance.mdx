## How to mark a register

1. Go to **Attendance** in the sidebar.
2. Open today's register for your stream.
3. Tap **Present / Absent / Late** for each student.
4. Click **Confirm register**.

Once confirmed, guardians of absentees are notified automatically.

## Notes

- Mark the morning and afternoon registers separately.
- Use **Mark all present** to speed up routine days.

## Troubleshooting

- **Student missing from register?** Confirm grade and stream on their profile.
- **Cannot edit a register?** It may already be locked — contact your Principal.
