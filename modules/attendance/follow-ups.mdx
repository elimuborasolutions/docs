A follow-up records the reason for an absence and any action taken. Each absence appears as a pending follow-up until closed.

## How to close a follow-up

1. Go to **Attendance → Follow-ups**.
2. Open the pending follow-up.
3. Pick a **Reason** (sick, family, late bus, etc.).
4. Add notes and (optional) upload evidence.
5. Click **Close follow-up**.

## Notes

- Evidence files (medical notes, letters) stay attached for audit.
- Closed follow-ups remain in reports.

## Troubleshooting
- **File too large?** Max 5 MB per attachment.
