## How to generate a report

1. Go to **Attendance → Reports**.
2. Pick the report type (per student, per stream, per term).
3. Set the date range.
4. Click **Generate**.
5. Download as PDF or CSV.

## Notes

- Reports include both morning and afternoon registers.
- Use term-level reports for parents' day prep.
