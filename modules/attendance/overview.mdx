Attendance covers daily student registers, staff sign-in, absence follow-ups, and reports. Guardians are notified automatically when their child is absent.

## Notes

- Registers are auto-generated each morning and afternoon.
- Class Teachers see only their own streams.

## Troubleshooting

- **No register today?** Check school is in session and not on a holiday.
