A notice is a one-off message — for example, "School closed tomorrow due to weather."

## How to send a notice

1. Go to **Events → Notices**.
2. Click **New notice**.
3. Enter title and message.
4. Pick the audience.
5. Click **Send**.

## Notes

- Notices appear in-app and (if enabled) by email/SMS.
- Past notices remain in the audit log.
