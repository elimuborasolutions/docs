## How to create an event

1. Go to **Events**.
2. Click **New event**.
3. Enter title, date/time, and location.
4. Pick the audience (whole school, grade, role, or specific list).
5. Choose reminder timing.
6. Click **Save**.

## Notes

- All-day events skip start/end times.
- Reschedules trigger a new notification.

## Troubleshooting

- **Wrong audience notified?** Edit and re-save; only new attendees are notified.
