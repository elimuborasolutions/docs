## Audit Trail

The system automatically logs actions such as creating records, editing existing data, and deleting items.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Log">
    Administrators can go to **Activity Log** to see a master list of all recent actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by Module">
    Filter the logs by module (e.g., Finance, Attendance, Users) to find specific types of actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Change Details">
    Click on an entry to see the "Before" and "After" states of the data. For example, you can see exactly which fee amount was changed on an invoice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## User Accountability

Every log entry includes:

- **The User**: Who performed the action.
- **The Action**: What was done (Created, Updated, Deleted, Logged In).
- **The Date & Time**: Precisely when the action occurred.
- **The IP Address**: (Optional) The network location of the user.

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can activity logs be deleted?">
    No. To maintain audit integrity, activity logs are permanent and cannot be modified or deleted, even by Super Admins.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long are logs stored?">
    Logs are stored indefinitely by default, providing a complete historical record for the life of your school's Elimu Bora account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
