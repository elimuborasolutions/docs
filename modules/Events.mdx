## School Events

Events are activities with a specific date and time, such as Sports Days, Half-Term breaks, or Exam periods.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the Event">
    Go to **Events** and click **New Event**. Provide a clear title, location, and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Schedule">
    Select the start and end dates. For multi-day events like "End of Year Exams," the system will mark the entire period on the school calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Audience">
    Choose who needs to know about this event. You can target the **Whole School**, specific **Grade Levels**, or individual **Roles** (e.g., only Teachers).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Reminders">
    Enable automated reminders to go out via SMS, Email, or App Notification (e.g., 24 hours before the event).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Events marked as **Holidays** or **Non-Academic Days** will automatically suppress attendance registers for those dates.
</Info>

## Urgent Notices

Notices are for immediate communication that doesn't necessarily require a calendar slot (e.g., "Reminder: School fees are due tomorrow").

<Steps>
  <Step title="Compose Notice">
    Navigate to **Notices** and click **Send Notice**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Target Your Audience">
    Just like events, you can filter your audience to ensure the message only goes to relevant people (e.g., only Grade 4 Guardians).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Delivery Channels">
    Depending on your school's plan, you can send the notice via:
    - **In-App Notification**: Appears on the mobile app and dashboard.
    - **SMS**: For urgent reach without internet access.
    - **Email**: For detailed announcements or attachments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send and Track">
    Click **Send**. You can view the delivery status and history in the **Sent Notices** log.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Audience Targeting

One of the most powerful features of the Events module is the ability to segment your school community.

- **By Grade/Stream**: Perfect for class-specific trips or parents' days.
- **By Role**: Internal staff meetings or administrative updates.
- **By Department**: Department-specific notices for teachers.
- **Individual Selection**: For specific student or staff disciplinary or merit notices.

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel an event?">
    Yes. When you cancel an event, the system will ask if you'd like to send a cancellation notice to the assigned audience automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I attach a file to a notice?">
    When composing a notice via **Email**, you can upload PDF attachments (e.g., a newsletter or a fee structure document).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why didn't some guardians receive the SMS?">
    Check the **Notification Log** for any delivery failures. Common causes include incorrect phone numbers or insufficient SMS credits on the school account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
