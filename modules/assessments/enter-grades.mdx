## How to enter grades

1. Go to **Assessments**.
2. Open the assessment in the **Grading** stage.
3. Pick your subject and stream.
4. Enter scores in the table (Tab to move).
5. Click **Save**.

Insert screenshot: Grade entry table with student names, score column, and comment column]

## Notes
- Scores must be within the assessment's max marks.
- Add a per-student comment for the report card if relevant.
- Save often — long sessions may time out.

# Troubleshooting
- **Score rejected?** Check it's between 0 and max marks.
- **Student missing?** They may be archived or in a different stream.
