## How to publish

1. Open the **Report Cards** list.
2. Filter to **Draft** cards for the term and grade.
3. Click **Publish all** (or open a card and click **Publish**).
4. Confirm.

Guardians receive an email with a PDF and an in-app notification.

[Insert screenshot: Publish confirmation modal listing the number of cards being released]

## Notes

- Once published, edits require unpublishing first.
- Republished cards email a new PDF to guardians.

## Troubleshooting

- **Guardians not notified?** Confirm their email is correct and notifications module is active.
