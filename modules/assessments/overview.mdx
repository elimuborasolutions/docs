Assessments capture student performance — tests, exams, projects, CATs. The lifecycle is: **Create → Grade → Close → Generate Report Card → Publish**.

[Insert screenshot: Assessments index showing assessments with status badges (Open, Grading, Closed, Published)]

## Common tasks

- [Create an assessment](/modules/assessments/create-assessment)
- [Enter grades](/modules/assessments/enter-grades)
- [Generate report cards](/modules/assessments/report-cards)
- [Publish to guardians](/modules/assessments/publish)

## Notes

- Closed assessments are read-only — open a new one to amend.
- Report cards aggregate one or more assessments per term.

## Troubleshooting

- **No subjects in assessment?** Confirm subjects are assigned to that grade.
