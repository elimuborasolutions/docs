Report cards aggregate assessment results into per-student performance reports.

## How to generate report cards

1. Close the source assessment(s).
2. Go to **Assessments → Report Cards**.
3. Click **Generate**.
4. Pick term, grade, and assessments to include.
5. Click **Run**.

## How to add remarks

1. Open a generated report card.
2. Add **Class Teacher** and **Principal** remarks.
3. Click **Save**.

## Notes

- Class Teachers add their remarks per student; Principals can bulk-sign.
- Cards stay in **Draft** until published.

## Troubleshooting

- **Subject score missing on card?** Confirm the source assessment has that score saved.
