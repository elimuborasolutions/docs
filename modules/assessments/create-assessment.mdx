## How to create an assessment

1. Go to **Assessments**.
2. Click **New assessment**.
3. Enter a name (e.g. "End of Term 1 Exam").
4. Select term, grade levels, and subjects.
5. Set max marks per subject and the grading scale.
6. Click **Save**.

## Notes

- Assessment moves into **Grading** once teachers can enter scores.
- Lock dates prevent late edits.

## Troubleshooting

- **Subject missing?** Re-check the subject is assigned to that grade.
