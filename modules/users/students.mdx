Each student has a profile with personal details, admission info, current grade and stream, guardians, and academic history.

## How to enrol a student

1. Go to **Users → Students**.
2. Click **New student**.
3. Enter full name, admission number, date of birth, gender.
4. Assign a grade level and stream.
5. Link at least one guardian.
6. Click **Save**.

## How to transfer a student to another stream

1. Open the student profile.
2. Click **Transfer** in the top-right.
3. Select the new grade and/or stream.
4. Confirm.

## Notes

- Admission numbers must be unique across the school.
- Use bulk import for large enrolments — see [Bulk import](/modules/users/bulk-import).

## Troubleshooting

- **Student missing from register?** Confirm grade and stream are set on the profile.
- **Can't find a former student?** Toggle **Show archived** in filters.
