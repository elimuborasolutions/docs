## Notes

- Each user gets a welcome email with login credentials.
- Roles control what users can see and do — see [Roles & Access](/roles-and-access).
- Guardians are linked to students; never delete a guardian before unlinking.

## Troubleshooting

- **Welcome email not received?** Confirm the email address and resend from the user profile.
- **Duplicate guardian?** Merge from the guardian profile menu.
