title: "Guardians"
description: "Add and link parents and guardians to students."
---

Guardians are accounts that represent parents or guardians. Each guardian can be linked to one or more students and gets read-only access to their children's records.

## How to add and link a guardian

1. Open the student profile.
2. In the **Guardians** section, click **Add guardian**.
3. Search for an existing guardian, or click **Create new**.
4. Set the relationship (parent, guardian, etc.) and primary contact flag.
5. Click **Save**.

## How to send a guardian's login

- Open the guardian profile.
- Click **Resend invite**.

## Notes

- One guardian can be linked to multiple siblings.
- Mark exactly one guardian as primary contact for billing.
- Guardian email and phone are used for notifications and receipts.

## Troubleshooting

- Guardian not receiving SMS/email? Confirm contact details are correct and the notification module is active.
- Linked to wrong child? Unlink from the student profile, not by deleting the guardian.
