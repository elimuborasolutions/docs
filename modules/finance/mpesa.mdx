Elimu Bora supports M-Pesa STK Push and guardian-initiated Paybill payments. Successful transactions reconcile to invoices automatically.

## How to send an STK Push

1. Open the student's invoice.
2. Click **Pay with M-Pesa**.
3. Enter the guardian's phone number and amount.
4. Click **Send**.

The guardian gets an STK prompt; on success the invoice is marked paid and a receipt is emailed.

## How guardians pay via Paybill

1. Lipa na **M-Pesa → Paybill**.
2. Enter your school's **Paybill number** (set in Settings).
3. Enter the **student's admission number** as the account.
4. Enter the amount and confirm.

The system reconciles automatically using the admission number.

## Notes

- Failed STK prompts can be retried.
- Set Paybill credentials in **Settings → Modules → M-Pesa**.

## Troubleshooting

- **Payment received but invoice still unpaid?** Confirm the account number matches the admission number; reconcile manually if needed.
- **STK timeout?** The guardian declined or didn't enter PIN — retry.
