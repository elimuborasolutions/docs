Each student has a digital wallet for canteen, activity, and small in-school purchases.

## How to top up a wallet

1. Open the student profile.
2. Click **Wallet → Top up**.
3. Enter amount and source (cash, M-Pesa, transfer from invoice overpayment).
4. Click **Save**.

[Insert screenshot: Student wallet panel showing current balance, recent transactions, and the Top up dialog]

## Notes

- Low-balance alerts are sent to the primary guardian.
- Refunds are recorded as wallet withdrawals.

## Troubleshooting

- **Balance wrong?** Check the transaction history and reconcile each row.
