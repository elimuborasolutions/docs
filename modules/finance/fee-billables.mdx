A billable is a fee line item — Tuition, Lunch, Bus, Books. Combine them to generate invoices.

## How to create a billable

1. Go to **Finance → Billables**.
2. Click **New billable**.
3. Enter name, amount, and category.
4. Tick the grades and terms it applies to.
5. Click **Save**.

## Notes

- Use categories to group billables on invoices and reports.
- Mark optional billables (e.g. transport) so they apply only to opted-in students.

## Troubleshooting

- **Billable not appearing on an invoice?** Confirm grade and term match.
