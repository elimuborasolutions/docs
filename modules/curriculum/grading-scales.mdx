Grading scales convert numeric scores into grades (A, B, C…) or performance levels (Exceeding, Meeting, Approaching, Below — for CBC).

## How to create a grading scale

1. Go to **Settings → Curriculum → Grading Scales**.
2. Click **New scale**.
3. Add bands with min/max scores and labels.
4. Tick the grade levels and subjects it applies to.
5. Click **Save**.

[Insert screenshot: Grading Scales editor showing rows for each band with min, max, label, and remarks]

## Notes

- One default scale covers most subjects; add specific scales only when needed.
- Bands cannot overlap.


## Troubleshooting
- **"Score outside scale"?** Extend the lowest/highest band or adjust max marks.
