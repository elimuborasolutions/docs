Grade levels are the rungs of your school (e.g. Grade 1, Form 2, PP1). They determine which subjects, assessments, and timetables apply.

## How to add a grade level

1. Go to **Settings → Curriculum → Grade Levels**.
2. Click **New grade level**.
3. Enter the name and order (e.g. Grade 5, order 5).
4. Click **Save**.

## Notes

- Order controls how grades appear in dropdowns and reports.
- You cannot delete a grade level that has students — archive instead.

## Troubleshooting

- **Grade not in student form?** Confirm it isn't archived.
