## Notes

- Pick the curriculum (CBC or 8-4-4) once during setup; switching later requires support.
- Configure grades and subjects before enrolling students.

## Troubleshooting

- **Grade level missing?** Add it in Curriculum → Grade Levels.
- **Subject not appearing in assessments?** Confirm the subject is assigned to that grade level.
