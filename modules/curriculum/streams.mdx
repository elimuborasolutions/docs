Streams are class sections within a grade — for example Grade 6 Blue, Grade 6 Red.

## How to add a stream

Go to **Settings → Curriculum → Streams**.
Click **New stream**.
Pick a grade level and enter the stream name.
(Optional) Assign a Class Teacher.
Click **Save**.

[Insert screenshot: Streams page with New stream modal showing grade and class teacher selectors]

## Notes

- A stream needs a Class Teacher to enable daily registers.
- Stream names must be unique within a grade.


## Troubleshooting
- No register for a stream? Assign a Class Teacher.
