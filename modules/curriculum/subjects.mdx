Subjects appear in timetables, assessments, and report cards.

## How to add a subject

1. Go to **Settings → Curriculum → Subjects**.
2. Click **New subject**.
3. Enter name and code (e.g. MAT, ENG).
4. Tick the grade levels where it applies.
5. Click **Save**.

## How to assign a teacher

1. Open the subject.
2. Click **Assign teacher**.
3. Pick the teacher and the stream.
4. Click **Save**.

## Notes

- A teacher can teach the same subject across multiple streams.
- Removing a subject from a grade hides it everywhere downstream.

## Troubleshooting

- **Subject not in assessments?** Confirm it's assigned to the relevant grade.
