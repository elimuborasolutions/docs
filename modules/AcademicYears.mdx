## Academic Years

The Academic Year is the highest level of organization in Elimu Bora. Only one year can be active at any given time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Academic Years">
    Go to **Settings** and select **Academic Years** under the Academic Structure group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Year">
    Click **New Academic Year**. Enter the year name (e.g., "2026") and the precise start and end dates for the entire year.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Status">
    Newly created years are set to **Upcoming** by default. When the year begins, the system will automatically transition it to **Active** based on the start date, or you can manually activate it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Once an academic year's end date has passed, it becomes a read-only historical record. This ensures the integrity of past school data.
</Info>

## Managing Terms

Terms divide your academic year into manageable periods for teaching and assessment. In the Kenyan system, this typically consists of three terms.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Year">
    Select the academic year you want to manage from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Term">
    Navigate to the **Terms** section and click **New Term**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Term Details">
    Enter the term name (e.g., "Term 1") and its specific start and end dates. Ensure these dates do not overlap with other terms in the same year.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Assessment Weights">
    (Optional) Assign weights to the term if your school uses weighted termly averages for final year-end results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Year-End Transitions

As the academic year closes, you must transition students to their next grade levels and prepare the system for the upcoming year.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Student Promotions">
    Move students to the next grade level:
    1. Verify that all assessments for the current year are finalized.
    2. Review the promotion list for each class.
    3. Manually adjust any exceptions (e.g., students repeating a grade or transferring out).
    4. Execute the promotion to update student profiles for the new year.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Financial Rollover">
    Ensure unpaid balances are carried forward:
    1. Run a final fee report for the closing year.
    2. The system will automatically carry forward outstanding balances to the new academic year's invoices.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I delete an academic year?">
    Academic years with existing records (attendance, grades, invoices) cannot be deleted to preserve data integrity. You can only archive them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I mark attendance for a new term?">
    Ensure the term status is **Active** and that the current date falls within the term's start and end dates.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I handle middle-of-term breaks (Half-Term)?">
    You don't need to create separate terms for half-term breaks. Simply mark those days as non-academic in the **Events** or **Holidays** module to prevent them from appearing in attendance registers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
