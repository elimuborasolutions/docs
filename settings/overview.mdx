## School Profile

Define the core identity of your institution.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Update Basic Info">
    Go to **Settings → School Profile**. Update the official school name, postal address, and phone number. These details appear on all generated invoices and report cards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Registration Details">
    Include your school's registration number and KRA PIN for official documentation and compliance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Academic Defaults">
    Set the default school hours and days of operation to help the system auto-generate registers correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Branding

Make the platform your own by adding your school's visual identity.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Logos & Icons">
    Upload your school logo in high resolution. This logo will be used:
    - On the login page.
    - At the top of all PDF invoices.
    - As the header for term-end report cards.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Report Card Styling">
    Customize the "look and feel" of your report cards. Choose between different layouts and color schemes that match your school colors.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Modules

Elimu Bora is modular. You can turn features on or off depending on your school's requirements.

- **Enable/Disable**: If your school doesn't have a boarding section, you can disable the **Hostels** module to simplify your navigation.
- **Custom Naming**: (Optional) Rename certain modules to match your school's terminology (e.g., changing "Streams" to "Classes" or "Forms").

## Notifications

Manage how you communicate with your school community.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure SMS Gateway">
    Link your SMS provider credentials to enable automated attendance and fee alerts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Templates">
    Edit the standard messages sent for:
    - Welcome messages.
    - Absentee alerts.
    - Fee reminders.
    - Assessment publication notices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Notification Rules">
    Choose which events trigger automatic notifications and which roles should receive them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my school name later?">
    Yes, but be aware that any documents generated *before* the name change (like old invoices) will still show the old name.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get more SMS credits?">
    Contact your Elimu Bora account manager or use the **Buy Credits** action within the Notifications module to top up your balance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
